Who Is This For

🚕

Robotaxi 运营商Robotaxi Operators

百度Apollo（萝卜快跑）、小马智行、文远知行等无人驾驶出租车服务运营商Baidu Apollo Go, Pony.ai, WeRide and other robotaxi service operators

应用示例：基于参考架构搭建远程运维中心，接入事件数据库训练AI异常处置模型Example: Build remote ops center using reference architecture, train AI models with incident database
🏭

整车厂（OEM）OEMs

上汽、一汽、长安、广汽等推出L4 Robotaxi方案的车企SAIC, FAW, Changan, GAC deploying L4 robotaxi solutions

应用示例：使用场景分类体系规划L4服务部署，参考KPI指标设计运营安全目标Example: Plan L4 deployment using scenario taxonomy, design safety targets with KPI benchmarks
🚀

远程运营创业公司Teleops Startups

如Vay、Ottopia等提供远程驾驶/远程协助技术方案的公司Vay, Ottopia and other remote driving technology companies

应用示例：在开源基线之上构建定制化产品，避免从零开始Example: Build customized products on open baseline, skip starting from scratch
📜

监管机构与研究者Regulators & Researchers

工信部、公安部、各地方交通管理部门，以及高校/研究机构MIIT, MPS, local authorities, universities and research institutions

应用示例：基于结构化事件数据制定监管政策，开展安全学术研究Example: Develop regulatory policies from structured incident data, conduct safety research
